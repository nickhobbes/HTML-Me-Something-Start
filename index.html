Event Details

LakeLAN is a annual 4-5 day event taking place in Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri. This is a regional level LAN event housing some of the most skilled Halo CE players in the country. Our goal is to keep this wonderful and unique game alive at a competitve level and bring in as many new players as possible. All skill levels are welcome.

LakeLAN 6 Tournament Details

Format:

  • Registered players are presented with a set of (10) wager chips upon arrival.
  • Wager matches can be played with any combination of players. There are no set teams.
  • All matches are 2v2 and consist of one best of 5 match. Games are first to 50.
  • Matches will be played on Halo CE NHE (Neutral Host Edition)
  • Wagers are initiated by Player A & B challenging Player C & D for a set number of chips.
  • Each player must put up the same amount of chips.
  • If a player runs out of chips, they may buy back in for $10 ($10 = 10 chips)


  • The 4 players with the most amount of wager chips by 8PM on Saturday will move on to the final round.
  • The final round will consist of round robin play with each possible combination of teammates.
  • The player with the best win/loss record at the end of the round robin will be declared the winner.
  • Runner ups will follow based on win/loss record. Tie breakers will be solved with head-to-head records.

Schedule:

  • Free Play will begin Wednesday at 8PM.
  • Wager matches will begin Thursday evening at 8PM.
  • The final round will begin Saturday at 8PM. All other matches will be stopped until the finals are concluded.

Registration:

  • Register by contacting Nick Cerame at nickcerame@gmail.com
  • Initial registration fee is $10 per player.
  • Players may buy back into the tournament for an additional $10.
  • All registration fees will be put towards the prize pool.

Prize Pool:

  • $400 + Registration Fees

  • Prize Split:
  • First Place: 50%
  • Second Place: 25%
  • Third Place: 15%
  • Fourth Place: 10%
